How to draw a water droplet!

First, why do we have to know?

Drawing water droplet can help you get better drawing skill and you can do it when you are bored. It also take little time to draw it when you get good at it.

Materials needed:

  1. pencil
  2. paper
  3. eraser

Step by Step Instruction:

1. Draw an ellipse

This ellipse will be the base of your water droplet. It can be circle or long ellipse...any way you want.

2. Add gradient inside the ellipse

It is shade of the water droplet. To do it easy, fill the ellipse with pencil, erase in a circle and rub it with your finger lightly until it looks good.

3. Add gradient outside the ellipse
